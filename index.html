JEWELRY MANUFACTURING: HOW IT WORKS

Jewelry manufacturing involves a series of steps from designing to the final finished product. Here's a general overview of the process:

1. MANUAL DESIGN

Manual jewelry design involves the process of creating jewelry pieces by hand, using various traditional techniques and tools.

2. cad

they are utilized in architecture, industrial manufacturing, product design, and, of course, jewelry!

3. rpt

3D printing has become a popular method for creating wax models in jewelry manufacturing.

4. casting

Casting is a very efficient method for producing large volumes of jewelry.

5. filing

Under Filing, the excess metal or solder is being removed from the piece.

6. polishing

polishing refers to processes that uses an abrasive that is glued to the work wheel

7. setting

The setting plays a crucial role in both the aesthetics and the overall durability of the jewelry piece.

8. Rhodium

Rhodium plating is used to create a white reflective appearance on jewellery.
